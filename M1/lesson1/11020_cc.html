Let's get started by first talking about what we mean when we talk about a Technology Readiness Assessment. A Technology Readiness Assessment is defined as a formal, systematic, metric-based process that assesses the maturity of technologies called critical technology elements to be used in systems. It's conducted by a group of experts called an Independent Review Panel.
