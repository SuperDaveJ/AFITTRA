Technology Readiness Levels

Text popups for each of the nine selectable TRLs on the TRL thermometer graphic display on the right side of the page in a framed text area.

TRL 1

In TRL 1, the basic principles have been observed and reported.

TRL 2

In TRL 2, the technology concept and / or application have been formulated.

TRL 3

In TRL 3, analytical and experimental critical function and / or characteristic proof of concept have been demonstrated.

TRL 4

In TRL 4, the component and / or breadboard have been validated in a laboratory environment.

TRL 5

In TRL 5, the component and / or breadboard have been validated in a relevant environment.

TRL 6

In TRL 6, the system / subsystem have undergone model or prototype demonstration in a relevant environment.

TRL 7

In TRL 6, the system / subsystem have undergone model or prototype demonstration in a relevant environment.

TRL 8

In TRL 8, the actual system has been completed and qualified through test and demonstration.

TRL 9

In TRL 9, the actual system has been proven through successful mission operations.
