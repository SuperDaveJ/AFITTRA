Mr. Norman Crow

Congress has oversight for the Technology Readiness Assessment process. The Milestone Decision Authority must certify to Congress that the technology in programs has been demonstrated within a relevant environment at program initiation.

The Milestone Decision Authority, also known as the MDA, must justify any waivers for national security to Congress. Because, historically speaking; there is little chance of a waiver being granted, it may not be something that the Milestone Decision Authroity wants to deliberate with Congress about.
