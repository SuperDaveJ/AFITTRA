Technology Maturity Factors to Consider

Technological Maturity Factors:

  • What is the required environment?
  • Does the technology provide enhanced or more robust capability?
  • Does the technology mitigate risk?
  • Did the original design include the new or current demands?

    What is the required environment?

    The technology must be tested in the correct environment which may be a relevant, operational, or, in some cases, combat environment.

    Does the technology provide enhanced or more robust capability?

    It’s not enough for the technology to work. It needs to improve upon what is already in use.

    Does the technology mitigate risk?

    Best practices should be implemented to avoid risk where possible.

    Did the original design include the new or current demands?

    The technology is mature according to design or the lack of design caused immaturity.
