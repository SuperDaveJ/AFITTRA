The key differences between the Early Evaluation of Technology Maturity at Milestone A and the Milestone B Technology Readiness Assessment are as follows:

  • For the Early Evaluation of Technology Maturity, the Independent Review Panel should include system-level generalists in addition to SMEs. This evaluation is not a replacement for the Milestone B Technology Readiness Assessment.
  • The candidate Critical Technology Element list should be based upon information from Broad Agency Announcements, Requests for Information, market surveys, actual results from government- or industry-funded efforts, and any initial system design concepts being considered by the program office.
  • Because multiple design / technology options may be available early in the program, the Program Manager should develop a potential Critical Technology Element list that includes technologies associated with all the options. The Independent Review Panel should use its collective expertise to review and refine this list and determine a preliminary technology maturity assessment, without using Technology Readiness Levels, for each Critical Technology Element based on requirements and environments specified in the Initial Capabilities Document or draft Capability Development Document.
