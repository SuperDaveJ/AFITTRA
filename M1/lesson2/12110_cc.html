Now that you understand what is involved in a Technology Readiness Asessment and who the stakeholders are, let's look at how TRAs are handled in AF contracts.

The Deputy Assistant Secretary, Contracting (SAF / AQC) is the responsible authority for entering into, approving, terminating and taking all appropriate actions with respect to contracts and agreements.

Risk-Based Program Management and Decision Making is a key element in managing complex programs, so it makes sense that source selection for these complex programs is also risk-based.
