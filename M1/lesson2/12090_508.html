TRA Process Stakeholders

The timeline graphic displays the month requirements on the first row and a description of the activities within those requirements in the second row. The timeline sequence is from left to right with the Milestone Decision on the far right. The month and activity information is as follows:

  • 12 to 24 months prior to MD: PM requests TRA
  • 12 months prior to MD: AQR and PM develop plan
  • 11 months prior to MD: AQR coordinates IRP team
  • 10 months prior to MD: PM identifies candidate CTEs
  • 9 months prior to MD: AQR and IRP review CTE list
  • 8 months prior to MD: AQR coordinates plan with DRD (ACAT ID and IAM only)
  • 8 to 6 months prior to MD: PM collects data for CTE assessment
  • 6 months prior to MD: IRP assigns TRLs
  • No later than 3 months prior to MD: PM and IRP Lead document TRA finding and coordinate with AQR; and for ACAT ID and IAM, with DRD.
  • No later than 2.5 months prior to MD: PM submits TRA to AQR
  • No later than 2 months prior to MD: AQR submits TRA to CAE; and for ACAT ID and IAM, with DRD.
  • Milestone Decision
