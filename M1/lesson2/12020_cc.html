Let's get started with this lesson by taking a look at the different stakeholders in the Technology Readiness Assessment process. Each stakeholder has an important job in ensuring the success of the Technology Readiness Assessment.

The stakeholders include:

  • Director, Research Directorate – known as the DRD
  • Deputy Assistant Secretary for Science, Technology and Engineering – known as the SAF / AQR
  • Deputy Assistant Secretary for Engineering – known as the SAF / AQRE
  • Program Manager – known as the PM, who works out of the Program Management Office or the PMO
  • Independent Review Panel or IRP Lead
  • IRP Team Members
  • Contractor Liaison
  • And Additional IRP Contributors

Select each stakeholder for additional information.
