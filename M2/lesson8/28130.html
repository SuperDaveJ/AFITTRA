TRLs and Contract Language

DoDI 5000.02

The PM shall include language in the Request for Proposal (RFP) advising offerors that:

(1) the government will not award a contract to an offeror whose proposal is based on CTEs that have not been demonstrated in a relevant environment and

(2) that offerors will be required to specify the TRL of the CTEs on which their proposal is based and to provide reports documenting how those CTEs have been demonstrated in a relevant environment (Enclosure 2, para. 6.c.(4)).
