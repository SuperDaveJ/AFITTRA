In addition to the pre-assessment kick off meeting, the Independent Review Panel will take part in meetings which provide pertinent, in depth information concerning the relevant and operational environments.

These meetings ensure that each member of the Independent Review Panel has an adequate understanding of the requirements, identified capabilities, software and system architectures, concept of operations, and concept of employment to properly define these environments.

These pre-assessment meetings also ensure that the Independent Review Panel has an adequate understanding of the critical technology element design details to enable them to evaluate how it will function and interface. Without such understanding, a critical technology element cannot be assessed as mature.
