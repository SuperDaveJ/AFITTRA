In addition to providing the technological status of a system, technology readiness levels play a major role during milestone reviews. There are three such reviews during the life of the acquisition program.

In order for a component or system to gain certification for a particular milestone, the critical technology elements of the component or system must meet a certain level of technology readiness.

Select each of the milestones on the acquisition phases diagram to view their corresponding required technology readiness levels on the thermometer.
