Once it has been determined that each Independent Review Panel member has an adequate understanding of the critical technology elements, it’s time to begin the assessment. Each member reviews, analyzes, and assesses the data and artifacts to determine each element’s technology readiness level, and then documents the results and rationale. The duration of the assessment period depends on the number and complexity of the critical technology elements.

The assessment consists of a series of meetings and takes place in a Program Management Office provided facility, where the security clearance level of the attendees is verified with the level of data to be presented.

During the assessment, there are several factors that the Independent Review Panel takes into consideration, and outside subject matter experts may be consulted to augment the group’s knowledge.
