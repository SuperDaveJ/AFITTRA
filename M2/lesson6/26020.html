Initial IRP Training

The creation of the critical technology element (CTE) list consists of three steps:

  • Step 1: Prepare an initial list of possible CTEs.
  • Step 2: Conduct a review to determine the final list of CTE candidates.
  • Step 3: Secure final approval of the list.
