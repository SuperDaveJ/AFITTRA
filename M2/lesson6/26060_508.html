What are the steps associated with finalizing the CTE list?

The steps associated with finalizing the CTE list are:

  1. IRP Team submits the finalized candidate CTE list to the IRP Lead
  2. IRP Lead submits the finalized candidate CTE list to the Component S & T Executive for approval
  3. Component S & T Executive submits his approved candidate CTE list to the DRD for approval
