The most important aspect of a team member is that he or she is a subject matter expert in a relevant field. As a subject matter expert, a team member will serve as the authority for a given technological capability area and help to enhance the team’s understanding of related processes new and old. To ensure that there is unbiased thinking, the team members should be independent of this program’s technology development.
