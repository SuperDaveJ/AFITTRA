As an Independent Review Panel Lead, I manage the assessment effort and schedule. I strive for consensus among the team members when performing the assessment, while trying to avoid group-think. Each member contributes their individual view, and by doing so, adds to the holistic view of the technology.

In addition to communication within the IRP team, I must ensure a high level of technical coordination and communication between the team and academic and business partners for additional expertise. This helps ensure that Independent Review Panel Team activities integrate with the overall direction and guidance from the Program Manager.

I also communicate progress and upon assessment completion, sign the final Technology Readiness Assessment document, and outbrief the Technology Readiness Assessment results to Mr. Vale.

Lastly, we are to document and submit the assessment to Ms. O’Neil and Mr. Vale for their review.
