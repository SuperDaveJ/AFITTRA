Mrs. Laura Palmer

It’s really straightforward; the functional architecture describes what the system accomplishes in quantitative terms. The physical architecture identifies the subsystems and components for the hardware and software that are necessary to realize the system concept and its functional architecture. By understanding both, you can better assess a system’s level of readiness.
