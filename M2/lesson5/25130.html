Systems Engineering Approach

 

 

Requirements Analysis

  • What are the operational requirements?
  • How well does the system need to perform to be effective?
  • What are the costs associated with these requirements?

Functional Analysis and Allocation

  • What does the system do?
  • Does it meet system requirements?

Design Synthesis

  • Are system technologies mature?
  • What kind of performance can be expected?
  • Can the technologies be manufactured?
  • What are the costs associated with the technologies?
