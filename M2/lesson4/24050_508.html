DAU CLE-021 Technology Readiness Assessment

DAU CLE-021 is a Defense Acquisition University course that covers the Department of Defense (DoD) philosophy regarding TRAs and is highly recommended as a prerequisite.

This online covers:

  • Technology and management factors used in the Critical Technology Element (CTE) identification process
  • Basic maturity characteristics associated with various levels of technology maturity
  • Requirements for preparing and reviewing a TRA
  • Technology maturation considerations
  • TRA best practices for both software and hardware systems

SYS-109 Technology Readiness Assessment

SYS-109 is an online course that covers:

  • Foundational knowledge about conducting a TRA
  • Activities associated with conducting a TRA

SYS-209 Technology Readiness Assessment

SYS-209 is an instructor-led course that covers:

  • When and why a TRA should be conducted
  • Roles and responsibilities of personnel conducting a TRA
  • Specific needs and requirements of a PM for planning and supporting required TRAs
  • How to plan, conduct, and participate on a team performing a TRA
