When Tab 5, Estimated Schedule, is selected, the page turns to display the Estimated Schedule page and plays the audio description of the schedule elements. The information on the Estimated TRA Schedule graphic displays as follows:

Estimated TRA Schedule

This graph-like table displays the following column headings across the top, from left to right: Task Number, Task, Month 1 through Month 12 (individual columns).

The information for each row displays as follows:

  • Task Number: 001; Task: PM, Component S&T Executive, & DRD agree on schedule; a triangle is positioned at the beginning of the Month 1 column.
  • Task Number: 002; Task: Component S&T Executive forms IRP; a bar is positioned from 5.3 months to 6.25 months.
  • Task Number: 003; Task: DRD concurs with composition of IRP; a bar is positioned from 6.25 months to 6.5 months.
  • Task Number: 004; Task: PM prepares possible list of CTEs; a bar is positioned from 6.5 months to 6.75 months.
  • Task Number: 005; Task: IRP establishes CTE candidates, iteratively with the PM; a bar is positioned from 6.5 to 8 months.
  • Task Number: 006; Task: DRD concurs with final CTEs; a bar is positioned from 7.8 to 8.4 months.
  • Task Number: 007; Task: PM compiles evidence of CTE maturity; a bar is positioned from the beginning of Month 1 to 9.5 months.
  • Task Number: 008; Task: IRP evaluates TRLs of CTEs; a bar is positioned from 9.5 to 10 months.
  • Task Number: 009; Task: IRP prepares TRA report body for Component S&T Executive; a bar is positioned from 10 to 10.5 months.
  • Task Number: 010; Task: Component S&T Executive prepares TRA cover memorandum; a bar is positioned from 10.5 to 11 months.
  • Task Number: 011; Task: Component S&T Executive sends TRA to CAE, with copy to DRD; a triangle is positioned at the beginning of the Month 11 column.
  • Task Number: 012; Task: CAE accepts findings or reconciles them with Component S&T Executive; a triangle is positioned at 11.25 months.
  • Task Number: 013; Task: CAE sends endorsed TRA to DRD; a triangle is positioned at 11.5 months.
  • Task Number: 014; Task: Milestone review meeting; a triangle is positioned at the end of 12 months.
