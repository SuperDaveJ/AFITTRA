Mr. Norman Crow

Title 10 United States Code 2366b sets the ground work for the initiation of a Technology Readiness Assessment with the statement, “the technology in the program has been demonstrated in a relevant environment”.

DoDI 5000.02 contains the requirements and procedures for ACAT programs. For programs with critical technology elements, that are ACAT I, or have the potential to become ACAT I, the initiation of a TRA is statutory, as mandated by Congress.

ACAT II and III acquisition programs have a regulatory mandate that requires the initiation of a Technology Readiness Assessment.
