Program Management Office (PMO) Program Manager (PM)

The PM initiates the TRA effort by contacting the Component S&T Executive and making him / her aware of prospective technologies that might be integrated into the Acquisition Program which require the TRA.  This is done no later than 12 months prior to Milestones B or C.

Component Science & Technology (S&T) Executive

The Component S&T Executive serves as the initial TRA process owner and works in concert with the PM to ensure that all elements of the TRA are addressed for the particular program.

The TRA in Action scenario popup appears when the TRA in Action icon is selected.

TRA in Action

After determining the need for a TRA, Jennifer contacts the Assistant Secretary of the Air Force for Science, Technology, and Engineering (SAF / AQR) office and notifies Cameron Vale, the Component (S&T) Executive, of the program’s need.
