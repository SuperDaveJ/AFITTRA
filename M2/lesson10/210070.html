What are the steps associated with staffing the TRA?

The steps associated with staffing the TRA are:

  1. PM submits signed TRA Report to Component S & T Executive
  2. Component S & T Executive performs peer review and submits TRA Report and cover letter to CAE
  3. CAE reviews and submits TRA Report, Component S & T Executive cover letter, and his / her cover letter to DRD
  4. DRD reviews and submits TRA Report, the two cover letters, and his / her Evaluation Memorandum to MDA
