The DRD Review

The DRD evaluates the TRA Report in cooperation with the Component S & T Executive and the PM. An Action Officer (AO), designated by the DRD, will normally lead the evaluation effort.

After an initial evaluation, the AO can either concur with the TRA Report and cover letters or request revisions. In the case where revisions are requested, the TRA Report will be updated and returned to the AO for further review once the revisions are completed.

If the DRD does not concur with the report after the revisions, an Independent Technical Assessment (ITA) may be conducted, but this decision outcome is rare.
